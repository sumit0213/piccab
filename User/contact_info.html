About PICCAB

At PICCAB, we believe in empowering both drivers and users by enabling them to determine the right fare for their journeys. Upon entering their pickup and drop-off locations, users are presented with the expected fare based on market rates. Subsequently, they broadcast this offer to nearby drivers, who then respond with their counteroffers. Users have the freedom to select the best offer from the received counteroffers.

Our primary goal is to minimize discrepancies in fare estimation, ensuring that both users and drivers can transact with confidence. By facilitating transparent communication and negotiation, we strive to create a platform where fair pricing is the norm.

Payment Model

In terms of payment, we operate on a subscription-based model for drivers. For a nominal daily fee, drivers gain access to unlimited ride requests, empowering them to maximize their earnings potential. It's important to note that PICCAB does not intervene in the fare negotiation process; transactions occur directly between drivers and users.
